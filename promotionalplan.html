Part 2: Promoting our Channel

Part 2 of our plan is how we would start on promoting. We plan on using posters and possibly making trailers/introduction videos about our streams but for now we’d like to start with posters. This is the poster we plan on using. We’ll be including a QR code to our channel and making a short introduction and overview of what our content is and what our channel would be like. We wanted this to be something easily accessible for our channel which is why we also included a QR Code for our poster in order to bring the future viewers to our channel easily.
